Biography Of Ghani khan:

Ghani Khan (1914–1996) was a renowned Pashto poet, philosopher, painter, and sculptor from present-day Khyber Pakhtunkhwa,Pakistan . He was the son of Abdul Ghaffar Khan and is celebrated for his philosophical, romantic, and humanistic poetry.
